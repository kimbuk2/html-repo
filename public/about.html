About Me

In my role as owner of our demo environments at Salesforce, I get to collaborate with amazing individuals on our Non-Profit and Education teams. I adore technology, but I'm also deeply committed to the arts and building an inclusive outdoor community. On the daily, you'll find me weightlifting, mountain biking, snowboarding, playing video games, reading, or more recently, experimenting with cooking.
